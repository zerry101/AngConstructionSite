Our Few Process

Step 1

Project inspection

We appreciate your interest in our services. To initiate the process of crafting your dream home, we kindly request a payment of Rs. 25,000/-. Upon receipt, our dedicated team will promptly arrange a visit to your proposed work site. During this visit, we will engage in a comprehensive discussion to understand your specific home requirements and carefully evaluate your budget. .

Step 2

Dwelling planning

Following the site visit and discussions, we will design your home to meet your requirements, considering plot size, shape, and budget. You will have the opportunity to visually experience your complete home through our advanced virtual reality walk-through, at no extra cost.

Step 3

Project Estimate

Upon your full satisfaction with the home design, we will provide you with a comprehensive quote for the proposed construction of your residence.

Step 4

Assessment

With your home design and our construction quote in hand, we kindly request your presence to review and finalize the contract. As part of this process, we extend a warm invitation for a half-day tour to visit our nearby ongoing and completed work sites. This tour will enable you to directly assess and evaluate the quality of our projects before making a final decision on the construction contract.

Step 5

Final Resolution

Upon engaging our services for home construction, we will diligently handle all necessary documentation, including any required bank loan paperwork. Additionally, we will proactively facilitate the acquisition of building permits and ensure the necessary GSECL supply from the appropriate government offices, ensuring a seamless commencement of your construction project.
